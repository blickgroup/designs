Meet the Team
Behind the Team

Blick runs on a quiet crew of AI helpers working around the clock. Here's who they are, what they do, and why they matter.

The Cast

You might not see them in meetings, but this crew is always on. They read, remember, watch, connect, and deliver — so the human team can focus on the work that actually needs a human.

Character 01
Nexus
The Brain

Nexus is Blick's living, breathing second brain. It reads every document, email, product note, and market update the company touches — then finds the connections that matter. Think of it as the team member who has read everything and forgets nothing.

A typical day
  • Indexes new documents as they arrive
  • Answers questions using company knowledge
  • Spots patterns across suppliers, competitors & products
  • Feeds insights into the morning brief
Sharp, curious, always reading
Character 02
The Briefer
The Morning Briefer

Every morning, The Briefer compiles the daily intelligence brief — industry news, competitor moves, product updates, anything relevant to Blick's world — and has it ready before the first coffee is made. Never calls in sick, never exaggerates, always cites sources.

A typical day
  • Scans industry sources overnight
  • Writes the daily intelligence brief
  • Highlights what's changed since yesterday
  • Files findings into The Vault automatically
Reliable, punctual, never misses
Character 03
The Watchman
Night Watchman

The Watchman never sleeps. While the team is offline, he quietly checks that every system, pipeline, and data feed is healthy. If something's wrong, he fixes it. If he can't fix it, he leaves a note so it's the first thing you see in the morning.

A typical day
  • Checks all systems every 30 minutes
  • Restarts stuck processes automatically
  • Flags anything that needs human eyes
  • Logs every incident, every fix
Steady, vigilant, calm under pressure
Character 04
The Vault
The Memory

The Vault is where Blick's knowledge lives. Every product spec, supplier note, meeting outcome, and market insight goes in — organised, searchable, and ready to recall. It's the company's institutional memory, always growing, never lost.

A typical day
  • Stores notes, specs, and research automatically
  • Answers questions from Nexus on demand
  • Flags knowledge that's getting out of date
  • Keeps everything linked and findable
Organised, patient, never forgets
Character 05
The Skills
The Specialists

The Skills are a bench of specialist helpers — each one trained for a specific job. Need a report reviewed? There's a skill for that. Need a bug fixed, a document drafted, or a design checked? You call the right specialist, they handle it, they hand it back done.

A typical day
  • Run automated reviews and audits
  • Draft documents and emails on request
  • Fix code and test it before handing back
  • Coordinate multi-step tasks end to end
Expert, efficient, always ready
Character 06
The Gateway
The Gatekeeper

The Gateway sits quietly between Blick's private files and the outside world. Whenever the team needs to work with a confidential document, the Gateway reads it locally — nothing sensitive ever leaves the building. It's the team's privacy guarantee, running silently in the background.

A typical day
  • Reads Google Drive files on the team's behalf
  • Keeps sensitive docs off external AI servers
  • Checks permissions before sharing anything
  • Works invisibly — you don't need to think about it
Discreet, principled, quietly essential
Character 07
The Wiring
The Plumber

The Wiring is the invisible plumbing that connects Blick's tools together. When a form is submitted, a notification sent, or data moved from one system to another — that's The Wiring doing its job. It runs hundreds of small tasks automatically so nobody has to do them by hand.

A typical day
  • Moves data between systems automatically
  • Triggers notifications and follow-ups
  • Runs scheduled tasks without being asked
  • Connects tools that don't naturally talk to each other
Hardworking, invisible, indispensable
Character 08
Hub
The Dashboard

Hub is where the team shows up for work. It's the internal platform that gives everyone a clear view of what's happening — rigs, jobs, inventory, health and safety, communications — all in one place, always up to date. If Nexus is the brain, Hub is the face.

A typical day
  • Shows live status across rigs and jobs
  • Tracks inventory, people, and communications
  • Surfaces alerts that need attention
  • Keeps field and office on the same page
Clear, calm, always up to date
